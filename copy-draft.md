### FAQ
**Q:** What is a Goodz?
**A:** A 65×65mm wooden card with an embedded chip. Hold any modern phone near it and it opens a link — usually music — directly in the browser. No app, no account needed.

**Q:** Mixtape vs. Custom — what's the difference?
**A:** Mixtape Goodz are ready-made cards you buy and load with any playlist link — great for gifting. Custom Goodz are printed with your own artwork and ordered in quantities of 50+ — for artists, labels, events, and brands.

**Q:** Will it work on any phone?
**A:** Yes. Works on iPhone 7 and later (hold the top of the phone near the card) and virtually all Android phones made after 2015 (hold the back). No app download — the chip is read by the phone's built-in hardware.

**Q:** Can I change the music after it ships?
**A:** Yes — if the link points to a playlist you control (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.), updating that playlist updates what anyone tapping the card hears. The card never needs to change.

**Q:** How long does shipping take?
**A:** Mixtape Goodz ship in 1–3 business days. Custom Goodz take approximately 2–3 weeks from artwork approval to delivery.

### FAQ
**Q:** How does the recipient tap it?
**A:** iPhone: hold the top of the phone near the card — a notification appears at the top of the screen, tap it and the music opens. Android: hold the back of the phone and it opens automatically. No app needed on either device.

**Q:** Can I update the music after it ships?

**Q:** What if they use a different streaming service?
**A:** The Goodz opens whatever link you put on it. YouTube Music links work for anyone without an account — great for maximum compatibility.

**Q:** How do I find my playlist link?
**A:** Spotify: open playlist → three dots → Share → Copy link. Apple Music: open playlist → three dots → Share Playlist → Copy. We have guides for all platforms on our Support page.

**Q:** What if it doesn't work on their phone?
**A:** Works on iPhone 7+ and virtually all Android phones made after 2015. If an iPhone user has trouble, check Settings → General → NFC is enabled. Our support team is here if anything goes wrong.
