Tapping Your Goodz

iPhone

The NFC sensor on an iPhone is on the back of the device, along its top edge, to the right of the camera.

Android

On Android phones, the NFC reader is usually located in the middle or upper back of the phone.

iPhone 11 & newer

  1. Grab your Goodz.
  2. Wake your phone, and hold the top edge of your iPhone directly against your Goodz for several seconds.
  3. Open the notification that appears to be taken to your content.

iPhone 7, 8, and X

  1. Wake and unlock your iPhone.
  2. Swipe up to open the Control Center.
  3. Click on the NFC icon to enable NFC scan mode.
  4. Hold the top edge of your iPhone directly against your Goodz for several seconds.
  5. Open the notification that appears to be taken to your content.

Android

  1. Ensure that NFC is enabled in settings.
  2. Grab your Goodz. Wake and unlock your Android.
  3. Hold your Goodz against the middle back of your phone for several seconds.
  4. Open the notification that appears, or follow the instructions on the webpage that loads to access your content.

  2. Close the camera app if it's open: it can interfere with NFC.
  3. Make sure your phone has adequate charge.
  4. Verify your phone's operating system is up to date.
  5. Restart the phone and try again.

The Mixtape: finding and loading your playlist link

  1. Find your playlist link on your favorite streaming platform using the guides below.
  2. Copy your playlist link.
  3. Paste your playlist link into the field shown on your Mixtape Goodz setup page.
  4. Click Submit.
