How It Works

Hold Your Phone to It. That's the whole thing.

The same chip in your phone that reads a contactless card at checkout — Goodz uses that to open music instead of a payment.

01

Get Your Goodz

Order ready-made from the shop or configure a custom run. We encode your link into the chip before it ships.

02

Hold a Phone Near It

iPhone: hold the top of the phone near the card. Android: hold the back. No app, no unlock needed.
