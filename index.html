A Goodz is a 65×65mm wooden card with an embedded NFC chip. When tapped to any modern smartphone, it opens a link — usually music — instantly in the browser. No app required.
Mixtape Goodz are ready-made cards you buy and load with your own playlist link. Custom Goodz are printed with your artwork (band art, event branding, album art, etc.) and ordered in quantities of 50 or more — ideal for artists, labels, events, and businesses.
Yes. NFC is built into all iPhones since the iPhone 7, and virtually all Android phones made after 2015. No app download needed — just tap and go.
Yes — as long as the link you used (e.g., a Spotify playlist) is updated, anyone tapping the Goodz will get the updated content. For Mixtape Goodz you can update your playlist anytime on Spotify or Apple Music without touching the card.
Mixtape Goodz typically ship within 1–3 business days. Custom Goodz orders take approximately 2–3 weeks from artwork approval to delivery.
