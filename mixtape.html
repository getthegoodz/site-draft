How Mixtape Goodz Works

Three steps.

01

Build Your Playlist

Make a playlist on Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, SoundCloud — or just grab any URL you want to share. A Bandcamp album, a SoundCloud mix, anything.

02

Order & We Encode It

Add to cart, paste your link at checkout. We print your Goodz and encode the link into the NFC chip before it ships. Takes 1–3 business days.

03

Hand It Over

Give it to someone. They tap it to their phone — music opens instantly. No app, no account, no friction. Just music.
