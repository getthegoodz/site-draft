The Process

From idea to your fan's hands.

You upload, we print and encode, they tap. Nothing in between.

01

Upload Your Artwork

PNG or TIFF, 300 DPI, RGB. Our builder shows a live print preview before you submit — you'll see exactly what lands on wood.

02

Link Your Music

A Bandcamp album URL, a CSV of your Bandcamp download codes (one per card), or any link you want. Your call.

03

We Print & Ship

UV-printed on basswood, chip encoded, shipped in 4–6 weeks from artwork approval. Boxed, counted, ready to sell.

What fans need to tap: iPhone 7+ or any modern Android · No app · Opens in their browser in seconds

Built for Bandcamp

Your Bandcamp album, made physical.

Bandcamp already sells your music the way you want it sold — to fans, at a fair price, with the download included. Goodz gives those fans something physical to go with it.

  • Link any Bandcamp album or track URL
  • Upload a CSV of your Bandcamp download codes so fans are buying your digital music and getting something physical
  • Works alongside your Bandcamp store, not against it
