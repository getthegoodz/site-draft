The Process

From Idea to Fan's Hands.

01

Upload Your Artwork

Use our self-serve builder to upload your album art, event artwork, or brand logo. We need 300 DPI, RGB, PNG or TIFF. The tool walks you through it.

02

Link Your Content

Paste a Bandcamp URL, upload Bandcamp download codes, or link to any URL — a landing page, a video, a Spotify album, anything your fans should see.

03

We Print & Ship

We UV-print your art directly onto basswood, encode each chip, and ship in 2–3 weeks. You sell them at shows, through your store, or give them away.
