# Goodz Site — Image Generation Prompts

All images should feel editorial, tactile, and music-forward. Dark backgrounds preferred for most shots. No text overlays. No stock-photo feeling. Think: indie record label, not tech startup.

The product is a **65×65mm square wooden card (basswood)** with a subtle embedded NFC chip. It looks like a small piece of art — a square coaster / album-art tile. It has a slightly warm, natural wood tone.

---

## Global Style Notes
- Lighting: moody, directional — one strong light source, deep shadows
- Color palette: near-black backgrounds, warm amber/gold accents, natural wood tones
- Feel: physical, analog, tactile — hands, surfaces, real environments
- No phones showing screens unless specified
- No corporate settings
